About History on Canvas

Introduction

History on the Canvas is a website for people who are interested in western art history and want to learn more about it. You can start from studying a long history of art by diving into the timeline section. If you love to challenging yourself, you can go for fun facts and art detective sections. These are two mini-games that we prepared for you. Fun facts is a game that you can guess the little stories behind the masterpieces. Art detective is more challenge, you need to find out the differences between the original version and modified version of the masterpieces. You can always use the menu at the upper left corner to navigate between different sections. Hope you will have a happy jorney in exploring art history.
